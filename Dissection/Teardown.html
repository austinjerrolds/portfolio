CASIO fx-260 SOLAR II

Austin Jerrolds - ECE 4140 Dissection

Teardown

Here is the calculator and its case

First I removed the six screws on the back of the calculator

Here are those screws removed

From here I removed the back plate exposing the PCB. Next I removed the plastic heat stakes by breaking them.

Now that the stakes are removed I can take the PCB out of the front cover.

Here you can see the membrane that "connects" the buttons to the PCB.

After taking out the membrane you can see the back of the buttons.

Those buttons just fall out and I am left with the empty front cover.

Here is the front of the PCB without the front cover.

Here is the back of the PCB. From here I can see the processor under the black adhesive that holds it in place.
