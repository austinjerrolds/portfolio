History

Calculators have been around long before processors were invented. The abacus was an early counting device said to be invented between 300 and 500 BC. The first handheld calculator was invented in 1967 by Texas Instruments. The CASIO fx-260 SOLAR II came out in 2017. It is a non-programmable, non graphing scientific calculator. The calculator has 144 math functions and is solar powered.
