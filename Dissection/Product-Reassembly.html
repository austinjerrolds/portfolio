Reassembly

The first step is to put the buttons back into the right spot.

Getting them in place was the hardest part of reassembly since you can't see where you're putting them.

Next we will place the membrane back on top of the buttons.

Then we will slot the solar panel, LCD, and PCB back in place. We can't replace the heat stakes that hold it in place so there will be issues with connectivity with the buttons.

Lastly, we will add the six screws that hold the case together.

And here we have a working calculator.
