CASIO fx-260 SOLAR II

Austin Jerrolds - ECE 4140 Dissection

The CASIO fx-260 SOLAR II is a solar powered calculator. It's main function is for handheld calculations and can be used for general math, algebra, and trigonometry. The goal of this dissection is to gain a better understanding of embedded systems and their design.
